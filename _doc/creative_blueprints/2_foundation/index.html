Why AttendSure exists

The Problem

  • ●Manual attendance is easy to game — buddy-punching inflates hours and erodes trust.
  • ●SOPs and rules live in scattered files nobody reads.
  • ●HR has no credible view of who validly attended.

The Solution

  • ✓Face-verified check-in — real human face is valid, non-human is invalid.
  • ✓SOP / rules library — one authoritative place for policy.
  • ✓SLA / compliance dashboard — validity obvious at a glance.
